Agri-Smart: Final Year Project

Agri-Smart is a mobile application designed to help farmers estimate the required fertilizer amounts for their crops by simply uploading a photo.

The app is fully functional offline and uses the Urdu language to ensure it’s accessible and easy to use for local farmers.

Since farmers are the backbone of Pakistan’s economy, this app aims to empower them with smart, accessible technology that improves crop yield by offering an economical alternative to expensive devices like GreenSeeker.
